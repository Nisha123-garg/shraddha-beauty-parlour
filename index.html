About Us

Shraddha Beauty Parlour is the hair & beauty Salon in haryana dist. of Kurukshetra, small town(Ladwa). It is the most organised and customer centric Parlour chain in Kurukshetra, small town(Ladwa). In Our principal goal is to deliver premium world class hair and beauty services in an environment that encourages growth, transparency and trust. We develop and maintain our teams ability on a regular basis to ensure that we are at the forefront of the industry. With the believe that excellence comes through leadership, guidance and training, our goal is to have a smile in every clients face when they leave our salon premises. We are a Parlour chain that has a diverse service offering, which includes hair, bridal makeup, beauty, mani/pedi and nail services.
